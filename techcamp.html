    Highlight, On 2024 Children Tech Camp.

    This Year’s tech camp was an enormous success, and the kids had a fantastic time learning about a variety of tech-related topics. In Robotics they tackled difficult but enjoyable projects such as designing smart homes, making smart doors and constructing a Robot Car. These hands on projects allowed them to understand the practical applications of robotics in real life, making them confident builders of the future.

    For the Kids in scratch, the kids showed immense creativity by developing their own games and animations. They built a thrilling endless dog runner game, a fast- paced geometry dash game, and even crafted their own impressive animations. Scratch gave them a solid foundation in coding logic and sparked their imagination in a very fun and engaging way.

    In web Development, the children excelled at building fully functional websites. They created stylish fashion websites, informative health care websites. Now, they have the skills to independently design and develop websites l, setting them up for future success in tech.

    We are so proud of how much they have accomplished during this six weeks training and the best part is that they can now comfortably do all these things on their own. Don’t worry if your child was unable to attend this year’s tech camp, next year’s tech camp is coming and it promises to be even bigger and better. And for our lovely kids who attended this year they are welcome to join us again to further expand their skills and explore new tech tools.
    A huge thank you to the amazing parents and guardians for your unwavering support and for trusting us with your children’s learning journey. Let’s get ready for an even more exciting adventure next year!
