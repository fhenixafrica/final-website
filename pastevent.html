    Summary of our 2-day Free Tech Employability Workshop

    On Day One, our trainers introduced participants to various tech tracks, providing a strong foundation for exploring future opportunities.

    Day Two featured inspiring sessions from industry experts like Mr. Udeme, who discussed different tech fields and job paths, and Mrs. Imaobong, who offered essential tips on interview prep, resume building, and professional conduct. Mr. Ukeme wrapped up with advice on building skills for self-employment and the importance of personal branding online.

    Thank you to our speakers and participants for an incredible event! Stay tuned for more ways to grow and succeed in tech.

    An Inspiring Day at Akwa Ibom Tech Week 2024!.

    Forging a Path to Innovation: Fhenix Africa’s Inspiring Experience at Akwa Ibom Tech Week 2024.

    The Fhenix Africa team experienced an inspiring and unforgettable day at Akwa Ibom Tech Week 2024! Held at the Ibom Golf Resort, the event was a dynamic gathering of tech innovators, industry leaders, and passionate changemakers. From insightful panels to inspiring keynote sessions, every moment was packed with groundbreaking ideas and discussions. We explored the latest advancements in tech, shared visions for the future, and delved into how we can address today’s challenges with innovative solutions. The atmosphere was electric, filled with energy and a collective passion for pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. One of the highlights was connecting with forward-thinkers from all walks of life. We exchanged ideas with tech enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and experts alike, each bringing a unique perspective and passion for driving impact. These connections reaffirmed our commitment to building a tech-driven future that empowers our communities and drives meaningful change across Akwa Ibom and beyond. Akwa Ibom Tech Week 2024 was more than an event – it was a catalyst for growth and collaboration, and a powerful reminder of our shared potential. Armed with new insights, partnerships, and inspiration, we’re ready to step forward and bring these ideas to life. Here's to a future of innovation and impact!
